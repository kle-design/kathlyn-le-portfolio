A Little About Me

hey again! i'm kathlyn

/cath • leen/

I'm a recent graduate from the University of Houston, where I studied in the Cullen College of Engineering. I'm majored in Digital Media with a focus on UX Design, aiming to build a career where creativity meets problem-solving!

Over the past few years, I've had the chance to intern at FinThrive, TA and manage at the UH UX Lab, and represent students at UXPA Houston and UX Coogs. I learned that what I care about most is figuring out why users do what they do, and designing experiences that actually make sense for them. Good design starts with listening, and I try to bring that into everything I create.

I believe design is less about making things look good and more about making things feel right. Whether I'm running a usability test, building out a prototype, or presenting to stakeholders, I try to keep the person using the product at the center of every decision.
